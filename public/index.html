Where trust and relationships form the foundations for every child’s successful growth
Within our independent specialist day school, and our clinically-informed children’s homes across Shropshire, we provide predictable, nurturing environments, where trust and relationships form the foundations for every child’s successful growth and human development.We meet each child exactly where they are; nurturing their confidence, resilience, and curiosity, whilst helping them to grow, learn, engage and prepare for the wider world, beyond Acorn Wood.

Education and care that works together
At Acorn Wood, education and care are inseparable. We understand that children grow and thrive when learning is delivered in environments which are psychologically safe, grounded in nature and nurture, and built upon trusted relationships. By integrating care and education, we support children to access education in a an environment that is carefully tailored to meet their needs, whilst building resilience, and developing the confidence and curiosity they need to succeed - both in school and in life.
